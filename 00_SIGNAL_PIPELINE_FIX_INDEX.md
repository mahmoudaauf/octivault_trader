## What Was Fixed

### The Problem
```
✅ Signals generated by TrendHunter every 5 seconds
✅ Signals normalized to TradeIntents
✅ Signals submitted to event bus
❌ Signals NEVER reached MetaController
❌ NO TRADES EXECUTED
```

### Root Cause
`AgentManager.meta_controller` was `None` because MetaController was created after AgentManager and never passed/set.

### The Fix
Three entry points were fixed to inject/pass MetaController reference to AgentManager:
1. **main_live.py** - Inject after creation
2. **run_full_system.py** - Inject in phase 7
3. **phase_all.py** - Pass during initialization
