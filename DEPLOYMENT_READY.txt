SUPPORT & TROUBLESHOOTING
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Common Issues:

Q: "decisions_count=0 after deployment"
A: Check logs for "[SignalFusion] Started async fusion task"
   If missing, check for import/initialization errors

Q: "SignalFusion failed to start"
A: Verify shared_state has:
   - agent_signals dict
   - add_agent_signal() method
   - lock for async safety

Q: "Signals not being fused"
A: Check agents are emitting signals
   Monitor logs/fusion_log.json for activity

Q: "Fusion task crashes"
A: Check shared_state thread safety
   Look for race conditions in signal reading
