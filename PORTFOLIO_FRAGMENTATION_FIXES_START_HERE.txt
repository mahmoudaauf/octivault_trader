                          ✨ FRAGMENTATION LEVELS ✨
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

HEALTHY
   └─ < 5 positions OR (< 10 with good concentration)
   └─ Position Sizing:       100% of standard
   └─ Consolidation Trigger: None
   └─ Status:                ✅ Keep trading normally

FRAGMENTED
   └─ 5-15 positions with even distribution
   └─ Position Sizing:       50% of standard
   └─ Consolidation Trigger: None
   └─ Status:                ⚠️ Caution - reducing new positions

SEVERE
   └─ > 15 positions OR many zeros
   └─ Position Sizing:       25% of standard
   └─ Consolidation Trigger: YES (if 2+ hours since last)
   └─ Status:                🔴 Healing mode - consolidating
