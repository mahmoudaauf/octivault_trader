## The Three Critical Adjustments Explained

### Adjustment #1: Volume in Scoring (Not Rejection)
**Original Approach:** Remove volume filtering entirely
**Refined Approach:** Include volume as 20% of composite score

**Why This Matters:**
- Low-volume emerging symbols with good signals get low score (not instant rejection)
- Adaptive to market conditions
- Professional standard

### Adjustment #2: Light Validation (Keep + Add)
**Original Approach:** Minimal validation (format only)
**Refined Approach:** Keep format/exchange/price + $100 sanity check

**Why This Matters:**
- Catches obvious garbage (spam pairs)
- Preserves good opportunities (no false negatives)
- Proper separation of concerns

### Adjustment #3: Separate Cycles
**Original Approach:** All mixed together
**Refined Approach:** Discovery (5 min), Ranking (5 min), Trading (10 sec)

**Why This Matters:**
- Discovery is research (slow)
- Trading is opportunity capture (fast)
- Ranking is periodic decision (stable)
