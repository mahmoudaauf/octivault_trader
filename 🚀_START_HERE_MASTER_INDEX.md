## The Professional Solution

**Three Critical Adjustments:**
1. Move volume to scoring weights (not hard rejection)
2. Keep light validation (format + sanity check only)
3. Separate discovery/ranking/trading cycles

- 80+ symbols discovered → 60+ validated → 10-25 ranked → 3-5 trading
- Quality-based selection (not random survivors)
